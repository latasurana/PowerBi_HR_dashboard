# PowerBi_HR_dashboard
HR Analytics Dashboard Project

📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

📌 Project Overview

•	Developed an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard using Power BI to analyze employee demographics, compensation, workforce distribution, and attrition trends. 

•	Identified key factors influencing employee turnover using data-driven insights.

•	Created an interactive dashboard to support HR decision-making and employee retention strategies. 

🎯 Business Objective

•	Analyze employee attrition across different dimensions. 

•	Identify high-risk employee groups. 

•	Understand the impact of salary and overtime on attrition. 

•	Help HR improve employee retention through data-driven recommendations.

🔍 Key Insights

•	Employee attrition rate is 16.08%. 

•	Employees aged 26–35 years have the highest attrition. 

•	Research & Development and Sales departments contribute over 94% of total attrition.

•	Employees working overtime are more likely to leave the organization. 

•	Employees with lower salaries have significantly higher attrition. 

•	Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives show the highest turnover. 

________________________________________

💡 Business Recommendations

•	Improve compensation for lower-income employees. 

•	Reduce overtime through better workload planning.

•	Focus retention programs on employees aged 26–35. 

•	Strengthen career development opportunities in Research & Development and Sales. 

•	Conduct regular employee engagement and satisfaction surveys.
